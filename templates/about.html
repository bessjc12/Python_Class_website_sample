A Little About Me



I'm pursuing the MS in Gov Analytics after being a enthusiastic early technology adopter for decades. I thought it was finally time to get smarter about what makes the background compute happen. I also want to be able to contribute to the quick revolution in the ability to take descriptive analytics to the next level. The contributions of computing power and speed is allowing us to get answers to our business challenges based in data. I currently reside in Wash DC to support the Defense of Department CIO's efforts to create a data analytic environment where insights can make business processes more efficient thereby saving tax payer dollars.

Personally I'm interested in the future of environmental sustainability initiatives. I want to live in the space between original ideas, the branding that makes them executable, and the technology makes them come to life.
